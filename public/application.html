Questions of Activity 1: IOT Applications

1. What is a IOT Application?
2. What are the main features needed?
3. What are some examples?

1. What is a IOT Applications?

a. IoT applications work with IoT devices to perform variety of tasks like edge computing, surveillance, generate real time insights from data collected.
b. The application layer sits above perception layer and network layer.

2. What are the main features needed?

1. Secured Communication
2. Cross platform
3. Interactive and user friendly UI
4. Seamless computing.
5. Data Security.

3. What are some examples?

1. Smart home
2. Health monitoring wearables
3. Environment monitoring
4. Traffic management
5. Agricultural management

Questions for Activity 2: Smart Building

1. What does smart means?
2. What are the main features of a Smart Building?
3. What are Smart Building scenarios?

What does smart mean?

In context of smart devices it means devices capable of making decision on its own based on algorithms.

What are Smart Building scenarios?

1. Smart Curtains
2. Automated lights.
3. Biometric security systems.
4. Roomba vacuum cleaner.
5. Temperature moderators for better lifestyle.

What are the main features of a Smart Building?

1. Centralised monitoring and control system
2. Security
3. User friendly control system
4. Sensors

Questions for Activity 3: Control Interface

1. What are the high-level features?
2. Who are the users?
3. What are the parts needed?

What are the high-level features?

1. Door wont unlock unless credentials match.
2. Curtains will open or close depending on light intensity.
3. automated home cleaning.

Who are the users?

1. People living in smart building.
2. Employees and executives in office areas.
3. Shopkeepers in supermarkets

What are the parts needed?

1. Fingerprint sensors and camera for biometric security.
2. DHT22 sensors.
3. LDR sensors for smart curtains.
4. Infrared and photocells sensors for vacuum cleaners.
